SECTORS OF EXPERTISE

Hospitality

SKETS studio is the leader in this space and has completed over 1150+ large scale Hospitality projects. Some of these projects involved both Architecture and Interiors.

Corporate/ Commercial

SKETS has completed over 450+ Corporate Interior projects with various reputed clients across the globe.

Resturant

SKETS has completed over 1000+ bars & restaurants. With a team of experienced Architects & Interior designers, SKETS can engage right from the Concept Phase until the completion of the Construction Documentation Phase.

Residential
